Favorite Winter things

Activities

A fun activity that I love participating in is sledding. Grab some snow gear and some friends and go to our local park that consists of hills. It usually ends up as us having a snowball fight or building a snowman and using our snow gear on the snowman. Another fun activity during the winter is when they put up the Christmas tree and lights in Chicago. The Christmas tree is humongous and is filled with joy and smiles from the public.

Hot Chocolate and Pozole

A typical winter dish my family usually makes during the holidays is pozole. It's a hispanic dish that is a stew based with hominy and meat typically pork. You can add many toppings to it as you please but its warm and very delicious. You can never go wrong with hot chocolate that is my to go drink during the morning or whenever I've been outside in the cold.

Christmas

A family tradition of mine is that we open our christmas gifts on the 24th which is Christmas Eve. It's a struggle to stay awake until midnight, but having good bounding time with relatives makes it up. As well going to church during the morning of Christmas Day it makes me have a stronger connection in my religion and faith in God. Coming home after church to have a huge feast with the family, favorite dish that they make is a drink called ponche. It's a warm beverage that consists of fresh fruits, guavas, apples and sugarcane. Sometimes it has dried fruits like raisins or prunes but I usually take them off.
