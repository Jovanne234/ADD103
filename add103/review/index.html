NBA 2k26

NBA 2k26 is a nba video game that consists of multiplayer or my career playing with AI's. It allows you to create a player and choose what position to play as. It is a team base game and any action can change the way the game goes.

Power Forward

The position that I mainly play is the power forward in short terms "PF". This position can be played in many ways, but I specialize in setting screens for my team or even shooting. There is competitive game mode that is ranked based, which I only play with friends. You can see how different people move the basketball and play as a team. Power forwards in 2k26 are extremely over powered at the moment because they can carry the team. As though, this position is very good not many people can play this build correctly. I've been playing this position for many years from the previous games.
